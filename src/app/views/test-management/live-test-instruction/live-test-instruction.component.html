Navigation

1. To select a question to answer, you can do one of the following:

  • Click on the question number on the question palette at the right of your screen to go to that numbered question directly. Note that using this option does NOT save your answer to the current question.
  • Click on Save and Next to save answer to current question and to go to the next question in sequence.
  • Click on Mark for Review and Next to save answer to current question, mark it for review, and to go to the next question in sequence.

2. For multiple choice type question:

  • To select your answer, click on one of the option buttons
  • To change your answer, click the another desired option button
  • To save your answer, you MUST click on Next
  • To deselect a chosen answer, click on the chosen option again or click on the Unmark button.
  • To mark a question for review click on Mark for Review.

3. To change an answer to a question, first select the question and then click on the new answer option followed by a click on the Next button.

4. Questions that are saved or marked for review after answering will ONLY be considered for evaluation.

Navigation

1. You cannot select the sequence of questions or the section. You must attempt each question as it appears on the screen.

2. You cannot skip to the next section unless the time allocated for a particular section is completed.

3. You must select the answer to the question in front of you before you can move to the next question. You cannot skip any question.

4. You will not be able to return to any question once you have moved to the next question.

5. If you end the test before its scheduled time, either intentionally or by mistake, you will not be able to see the un-attempted questions on your test.

6. For each question, there is only one correct answer.
